### Owner-Occupiers

Generally insulated from taxation changes, but benefit indirectly from reduced foreign competition in established housing markets. Must be careful if utilizing part of the home for income-generating activities to protect full CGT exemptions.

### Retail Investors (Individuals)

Faced with higher compliance costs regarding minimum property standards. Reduced yield flexibility due to strict 12-month caps on rent reviews. Cash flow modeling is now vastly more important than relying purely on capital growth.

### SMSF Trustees

Navigating severe penalties for non-arm's length expenditure (NALE). While commercial property within SMSFs remains largely unchanged, residential holdings require rigorous documentation and independent valuations.

### Corporate Investors & Trusts

Subject to tightening land tax thresholds across various states, particularly regarding absentee owner surcharges and the aggregation of land values across multiple entities.
